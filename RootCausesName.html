Root Cause #2

Impulsive shopping habits can lead to financial distress and a poor credit card score.

Evidence

Organizations may already be using proprietary formats. [1]

Explantion

Those formats may not be supported, or poorly supported, by open source software.

Evidence

Resources are needed to switch. [1]

Explantion

It takes financial and human resources to support the switch away from existing software.

Implications

This issue can be solved with more common usage of open standards. Therefore, formats can continue to be used both internally and externally for an organization, and less effort is required to migrate.
